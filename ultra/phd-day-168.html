Ultrarunning Newcomer Will Run Across America Trying to Break 35-Year-Old Record

An ultra marathon is any race longer than a marathon (26.2 miles). Ultras can be run on roads, trails, or tracks. There are two types of events: those in which runners set out to cover a fixed distance (50 kilometers, 100 miles, etc.) and those in which runners attempt to cover the greatest possible distance within a fixed period of time (such as 24 hours, 48 hours, or six days).
