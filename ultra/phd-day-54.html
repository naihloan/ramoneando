Honey Walnut Oats

Ingredients

  • ¼ C. quick cooking oats
  • ½ C. skim milk
  • 1 tsp flax seeds
  • 2 tbsps chopped walnuts
  • 3 tbsps honey
  • 1 banana, peeled

Directions

For 3 mins microwave: banana, oats, honey, milk, walnuts, and flax. Stir the contents before serving in bowls. Enjoy.
