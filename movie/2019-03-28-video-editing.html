Choices to select a program and start editing videos

  • 5 best of 2019 [lifewire] [Non Professional options]
    • VidCutter: very simple cutter and merger (limited use)
    • Shotcut: 4K audio mixing (need to learn plenty)
    • OpenShot: simple effective transitions titles (slow n crash)
    • Flowblade: simple filters (no animated titles)
    • Kdenlive: easy (no titles slow kde)
  • 9 best video editing for linux [It's FOSS]
    • Lightworks: professional intuitive easy royalty-free content ~4K (limited free)
    • Flowblade: quick installs easy effects filters
    • DaVinci Resolve: professional video and audio post-production (not for average editing)
    • Cinelerra: professional HD (not for beginners)
    • Blender: professional but complicated (for 3D animation mainly, not regular editing)
  • Top 12 [Filmora]
    • Pitivi: simple intuitive audio keyboard-friendly
    • Vivia: friendly easy multi-camera support crash-recovery
    • Avidemux: codec friendly
    • LiVES: Video Jockey software
