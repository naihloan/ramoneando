Time Boxing

The Method Elon Musk Uses to Manage His Time | Thomas Frank Published on Apr 21, 2018

I am not very used to scrunching time to get to goals. But late as I am with completing some paper work and results to write quicker, it may be a good idea to asign daily, weekly, monthly benchmarks to reach. According to Parkinson's Law:

Work expands to fill the time alloted to it.

Time boxing:

  1. Removes choice from the equation.
  2. The time limitation intensifies your level of focus.
  • Do now [android app] / or ... a calendar
  • Split your bigger tasks into smaller sub-tasks.
  • Avoid over-schedule.
