Abstract

This practice-based investigation offers an understanding of the role of the body in drawing; proposing that drawing is not only connected to movement but can be located in a larger inquiry into the performative nature of human activity. The aim of the inquiry is to demonstrate how the body as an instrument can be explored through the malleable qualities of mark making.
