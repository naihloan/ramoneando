I’ve written in a journal almost daily since I was six years old. When I was back home in Oregon the other weekend, I flipped through one from the 3rd grade. In response to the writing prompt, “What do you want to be when you grow up?”, I wrote “a good person.” To this day, I can’t think of any better answer.

Because while I can admire someone’s accomplishments, I only respect them for their character - a thought that I’ve lately been keeping as a guidepost for myself.
