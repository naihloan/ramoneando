TRAIL ELS BASTIONS ¬Æ

76 Km 5.497m+

Description:

A circular race of 76Km with a positive and negative height difference of 5.497m and starting and finishing at Ribes de Freser. This is a trail that takes the runners to the highest peaks of the Vall de Ribes, the Puigmal, Eina, Noufonts, Noucreus, Torreneules, Balandrau and Taga.

It is an ideal race for those wanting to start out on long distance trails or wishing to test themselves before the main event.

The most part of the race is at an altitude of over 2.000m with an average height of 2.057m and the highest point is at the peak of Puigmal (2.910m).

The start will be at the Ribes de Freser municipal Sports Hall at 6 AM on Saturday 1 June 2019, and the race will finish at the same point.

The trial is limited to 300 runners.

The trial is certified by the ITRA with the following score:

  • Endurance points: 4 (new scale)
  • Mountain level: 10
  • Finisher level: 420

Entry price:

  • From 01/01/2019 to 15/04/2019: 80 euros
  • From 16/04/2019 to 30/04/2019: 100 euros

Other services:

  • Cancellation insurance: 10 euros
  • Eco cup: 2 euros to refill with liquids at the refreshment points.
